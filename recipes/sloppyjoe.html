Classic Sloppy Joe Recipe

This is a classic sloppy joe recipe. It's a quick and easy meal that is perfect for busy nights.

    Ingredients!

  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 1 can of sloppy joe mix
  • Cheese slices (Optional)
  • One pack of hamburger buns
  • Your choice of sides (Tater-tots, veggies, etc)

      • Directions!

  • Start by browning the ground beef in a skillet over medium heat
  • Once the beef is browned, drain the excess fat
  • Add the sloppy joe mix to the skillet and stir until the beef is coated
  • Let the mixture simmer for 10-15 minutes
  • Toast the hamburger buns in the oven or on the stove
  • Once the buns are toasted, spoon the sloppy joe mixture onto the buns
  • Add a slice of cheese on top of the mixture (Optional)
  • Enjoy your sloppy joes with your choice of sides!
